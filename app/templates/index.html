Turning tables that are really long or that have rows that are really long can be hard.

And some things you just can't do all that well because there are known issues with the technologies.

Like repeating the headers of the table on subsequent pages.

If you have a page-break-inside: avoid on the <tr>, then the repeating header will work just fine with wkhtmltopdf.

WkHtmlToPdf is significantly better at handling tables that go over a page break

But if you do not tell it to avoid splitting a row across pages, then the header will repeat but it will be placed over top of the content of the row and will be illegible. This is a known bug and does not yet have a good fix.
